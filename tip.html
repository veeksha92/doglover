DAILY CARE TIPS FOR YOUR DOG

On your computer, give these cards a quick hover to learn more!
On phones or tablets, just a friendly tap will do the trick!
Quick Reference Table

1. GROOMING: GLAM SQUAD FOR YOUR PUP!

Brushing

Spa Day!

Brushing your dog's coat helps remove loose fur and prevents painful mats. Short-haired dogs need brushing 2-3 times a week, while long-haired breeds require daily attention.

Dental Care

Fresh Breath,no Stank!

Brush your dog's teeth 3-4 times a week using dog-safe toothpaste to prevent bad breath and dental disease.

Paw Care

Paw Patrol!

Wipe your dog's paws after walks to remove dirt, chemicals, or salt that could irritate their skin.

Bathing

Bath Time Magic!

Bathe your dog every 4-6 weeks with a gentle dog shampoo to keep their coat clean without stripping natural oils.

2. EXERCISE: FUN & FITNESS CONTROL!

Daily Walks

Adventure Time!

Take your dog for daily walks– small breeds need 20-30 minutes, while larger, energetic dogs require at least an hour of exercise.

Playtime

Play lika a pro!

Engage in active play sessions like fetch, tug-of-war, or frisbee to burn off energy and strengthen your bond.

Training

Tricks and Treats!

Practice short training sessions (5-10 minutes daily) to reinforce commands and keep their mind sharp.

Socialization

Social Buterrfly?

Socialize your dog regularly with other friendly pups to build confidence and good manners.

3. FOOD HYDRATION: FUEL FOR THE ZOOMIES!

Meal Schedule

No Hungry Moments!

Stick to a consistent feeding schedule – puppies need 3-4 small meals daily, while adult dogs thrive on 1-2 meals at the same times each day.

Water

Life juicy!

Always provide fresh, clean water and refill the bowl at least twice daily to encourage proper hydration.

Healthy Treats

Do not spoil!

Limit treats to 10% of their daily calories and choose healthy options to avoid weight gain.

Diet

Human Food? NOPE!

Never feed your dog harmful human foods like chocolate, grapes, onions, or anything containing xylitol.

4. HEALTH CHECKS: BE YOUR DOG'S SUPERHERO!

Ears & Eyes Scan

Clear & Clean!?

Check your dog's eyes and ears weekly for redness, discharge, or unusual odors that could indicate infection.

Coat Checkup

Shiny!?

Run your hands through their coat regularly to feel for lumps, bumps, or skin irritations that need attention.

Paw Inspection

Paw patrol!

Inspect their paws daily for cuts, cracks, or foreign objects stuck between their pads.

Behavior Watch

Weird Vibes?

Notice changes in behavior or appetite, as these can be early signs of illness that require veterinary care.

5. POTTY ROUTINES: AVOID ACCIDENTS!

Puppy Potty Marathon

Tiny Bladders!

Take puppies outside every 1-2 hours since their small bladders can't hold it for long.

Adult Dog Breaks

Pooty Breaks!

Adult dogs need 3-4 bathroom breaks daily – don't make them wait too long between trips outside.

Poop Patrol Duty

Poop Patrol!

Clean up waste immediately to keep your yard sanitary and prevent the spread of parasites.

Celebration Time

Praise!

Praise and reward your dog when they eliminate in the correct spot to reinforce good habits.

6. BONDING: BEST PART OF THE DAY!

Cuddle Therapy

Cuddles!

Spend quality time cuddling every day to strengthen your emotional connection.

Communicate

Chatty Besties!

Talk to your dog often – they may not understand words, but they love hearing your voice.

Play Their Favorite Game

Play Time!

Learn your dog's favorite games and make time to play together regularly.

Quiet Companionship

Quiet Time Counts Too!

Include your dog in quiet activities like watching TV or reading – they just want to be near you!
