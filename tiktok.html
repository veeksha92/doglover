FAMOUS DOGS ON TIKTOK

1. TUCKER, THE GOLDEN RETRIEVER

@tuckerbudzyn

  • Dog Name: Tucker
  • Breed: Golden Retriever
  • Followers: 11+ million
  • Likes: 270+ million
  • How He Got Famous:
    • Viral for goofy expressions, funny inner voice narration, and taste-testing videos.
    • Edited subtitles and sassy voiceovers by his human mom.
    • Relatable skits, grooming mishaps, and family moments.
  • Unique Trait: His "dog thoughts" voice is a mix of clever, spoiled, and adorable.

2. BUNNY, THE SHEEPADOODLE

@whataboutbunny

  • Dog Name: Bunny
  • Breed: Sheepadoodle
  • Followers: 8+ million
  • Likes: 240+ million
  • How She Got Famous:
    • Bunny became a sensation for using AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) buttons to “talk” to her owner.
    • She was part of a scientific experiment and often used 40+ buttons to communicate full phrases like “outside now” or “why dog?”
  • Unique Trait: Smart and expressive; often asked existential questions!

3. MAYA, THE SAMOYED

@mayapolarbear

  • Dog Name: Maya
  • Breed: Samoyed
  • Followers: 5+ million
  • Likes: 84+ million
  • How She Got Famous:
    • Maya’s fame came from her ultra-fluffy appearance, expressive reactions, and adorably lazy behavior.
    • Popular content includes “Maya ASMR,” treat testing, and silly skits.
  • Unique Trait: Angelic fluff + always looks mildly annoyed but cute.

4. DOUG, THE PUG

@itsdougthepug

  • Dog Name: Doug
  • Breed: Pug
  • Followers: 6+ million
  • Likes: 104+ million
  • How He Got Famous:
    • Doug started on Instagram and went viral for celebrity-level costume cosplays and appearances with stars.
    • On TikTok, Doug's charm includes memes, music videos, and “daily Doug” vibes.
  • Unique Trait: A pug with an entertainment brand — even won a People’s Choice Award.

5. DEXTER, THE SPANIEL

@dexterdogouray

  • Dog Name: Dexter
  • Breed: Spaniel
  • Followers: 1.4+ million
  • Likes: 60+ million
  • How He Got Famous:
    • Dexter walks on two legs like a human after losing use of his front legs in an accident.
    • His story is inspirational and people admire his determination and recovery.
  • Unique Trait: Bipedal walking dog — looks like a man in a dog costume!
