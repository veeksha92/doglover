DIVE INTO FUN FACTS ABOUT DOGS

Labrador Retriever

Labs are expert food sniffers; they've even been known to open refrigerators! Because of how strong their sense of smell is, some diabetic alert dogs are used. Basically, you can never be sure that your snacks are safe.

Border Collie

They can create their own games and pick up tricks in a matter of seconds. They'll start sorting your socks by color if you don't keep them entertained. I mean it.

Poodle

Some poodles can learn hundreds of words because they are so intelligent. Beneath all that fluff is a brain that, with enough rewards, could likely complete your homework.

French Bulldog

They snore louder than most humans thanks to their smushed-up noses. It’s weirdly adorable… until it’s 3 a.m.

Beagle

Beagles are escape artists with a penchant for adventure. A Beagle will find a way out if there is one, usually while looking for a sandwich or an intriguing scent.

Shih Tzu

Despite being bred to warm the laps of Chinese royalty, these little fluffy creatures still demand VIP treatment. If they aren't allowed to take a nap on the most luxurious cushion, they will give you the side-eye.

Dachshund

These long pups don't realize how small they are. They will attempt—and succeed—to dominate dogs ten times their size. An added bonus is that their dramatic floor flops are hilarious.

Boxer

Boxers never truly mature. They act as though they've just had five cups of coffee, even as adults. Zoomies? Always.

Siberian Husky

Huskies scream, yodel, and sass-talk instead of barking. You'll know if they're not getting what they want. Quite loudly.

Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Corgis have a fluffy back that jiggles when they walk and no tail. Fun fact: Queen Elizabeth owned more than thirty of them. Obviously, even royalty enjoys a good wiggle.

Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

These puppies will gaze affectionately at you for hours on end. It's both endearing and unnerving. They will follow you about like a fluffy, soft shadow.

Miniature Schnauzer

They appear to be serious detectives thanks to their trademark mustache, but in reality, they are silly watchdogs who enjoy playing more than solving mysteries.
