DOG FASHION TRENDS 2024-2025

On your computer, give these cards a quick hover to learn more!
On phones or tablets, just a friendly tap will do the trick!

1. OUTFITS

TAILORED CHIC

Tailored styles are dominating, with tweed blazers, corduroy overalls, and miniature denim jackets offering a sophisticated look for small breeds like Chihuahuas and Pomeranians.

BOHO PUPS

Bohemian-inspired outfits, such as flowy linen rompers and crochet vests, are popular for summer photoshoots, especially on breeds with long coats like Shih Tzus.

ATHLEISURE UPGRADE

Athleisure wear has expanded beyond hoodies to include moisture-wicking polo shirts, jogger-style leggings, and even tiny sneakers for active dogs.

WEATHER-READY WEAR

Functional outerwear, like quilted puffer vests and waterproof barn coats, is essential for larger breeds such as Labradors during winter.

2. COLLARS & HARNESSES

RED-CARPET READY

Luxury collars made of Italian leather or silk-satin ribbons are trending for formal occasions, often personalized with engraved nameplates.

SMART & STYLISH

Tech-integrated accessories, such as GPS-enabled leather collars and LED-reflective harnesses, combine safety with style for urban dogs.

3. ACCESSORIES

HEAD-TURNING HEADWEAR

Headwear has evolved from basic bandanas to structured pieces like knit beanies with ear holes, fascinators for weddings and UV-protective "doggles".

PAW PROTECTION PERFECTED

Shoes now range from grippy rubber booties for city walks to hiking-specific footwear with Vibram soles for adventurous breeds like Huskies.

4. SEASONAL HIGHLIGHTS

SUMMER BREEZES

Spring/Summer 2025 favors lightweight fabrics like chiffon and mesh, with trends like tropical-print rash guards and cropped denim vests.

WINTER WARMTH

Fall/Winter 2024 leans into cozy layers, including cable-knit turtlenecks, shearling-lined aviator jackets, and Fair Isle sweater sets.
