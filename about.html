About Me

Hi there! I'm a passionate dog lover and aspiring cybersecurity professional who created this website in 2025 as part of my university coursework.

This site was born out of a module project in my Cyber Security program, where I was given the opportunity to combine creativity with technical skills. Instead of just building a standard site, I chose something close to my heart — dogs!

From fun facts and TikTok-famous pups to DIY crafts and safety tips, this website reflects my love for all things canine. It also gave me the chance to apply HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and design principles in a real-world project setting.

I hope this site brings a smile to your face, maybe teaches you something new, and reminds you why dogs are truly people’s best friend.

Thank you for visiting and making it to the tail end of the site! If you'd like to know more about my cybersecurity journey or projects, feel free to check out my coursework Check out my cyber journey!.
